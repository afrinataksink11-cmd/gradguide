Why we built this

High school students often face difficulties during the college counselling and application process due to the overwhelming amount of information available online, differing admission requirements across universities, and limited access to personalised guidance. Many students spend hours researching universities, scholarships, deadlines, and application requirements across multiple websites, which can be confusing and time-consuming. School counsellors are often responsible for supporting a large number of students, making it difficult to provide individualised advice to everyone. As a result, students may miss important deadlines, overlook suitable opportunities, or make decisions without fully understanding their options.

What we believe

Every student deserves guidance that fits who they are — their interests, their academic level, and their goals — not a one-size-fits-all experience. We believe the right information, surfaced at the right moment, turns a stressful, isolating process into a clear and confident one. Good guidance should give every student the kind of attention a great counsellor would, without the limits of time or caseload.

What we stand for

Personal by default

Advice is shaped by each student’s profile — never a generic checklist.

Everything in one place

Schools, scholarships, courses and deadlines together, so nothing slips.

Free where it matters

The essentials stay free; we only charge for deeper, human support.

Our mission

To build a college guidance platform that centralises application information, provides personalised university and scholarship recommendations, tracks deadlines, and offers step-by-step guidance throughout the application journey — helping students make more informed decisions while reducing stress and improving access to college counselling resources.
